Why I Love Potato Fritters

It's a homemade potato dish that’s both crispy and yummy.

Because of its unique taste, it has become my all-time favorite snack.

I only eat it when it’s freshly made at home!

Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥔 Potatoes
  • 🧂 Salt
  • 🌶️ Chilly
  • 🌿 Green Coriander
  • 🌾 Gram Flour

Steps to Make Potato Fritters

  1. Peel and slice the potatoes 🥔
  2. Mix all the ingredients together 🥣
  3. Heat the oil in a pan 🍳
  4. Drop the potato slices in hot oil one by one
  5. Fry until golden and crispy 🔥
  6. Serve hot and enjoy 😋

🎉 Your delicious fritters are ready to eat!
