A homemade delight that's absolutely irresistible!

✨ Why I Love Potato Fritters

It's a homemade potato dish that’s both crispy and delicious.

With its unique taste and aroma, it's my all-time favorite snack!

I enjoy them fresh and hot, just the way they’re meant to be!

🧂 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥔 Potatoes
  • 🧂 Salt
  • 🌶️ Chilly
  • 🌿 Green Coriander
  • 🌾 Gram Flour

👨‍🍳 Steps to Make Potato Fritters

  1. Peel and slice the potatoes 🥔
  2. Mix all the ingredients together 🥣
  3. Heat oil in a pan 🍳
  4. Drop potato slices one-by-one into hot oil
  5. Fry until golden and crispy 🔥
  6. Serve hot and enjoy 😋

🎉 Your delicious fritters are ready to eat!
